The actual problem

Restaurants lose money when the operation lives in people’s heads.

A missing prep note, a late 86, a forgotten closeout, a bad cash count, or an untrained new hire can look small in the moment. Stack enough of them together and the shift starts leaking time, product, and trust.

86 Chaos is built to keep the restaurant’s daily memory in one place, so the team can move faster without relying on one overworked person to remember everything.

FAQ

Questions owners will ask before they scan the menu.

Is this a POS system?

No. 86 Chaos is an operations hub. POS integrations are planned, but the app is designed to work with manual closeout and report imports first.

Is the data in these screenshots real?

No. The marketing mockups use realistic sample restaurant data. Do not publish private employee, customer, wage, or sales data on a public page.

Who is it built for?

Independent restaurants, bars, grills, cafés, and kitchens that need better shift control without a giant corporate system.

What happens after beta?
