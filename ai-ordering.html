AI Order Assistant

Smarter orders from stock, prep, waste, events, and invoices.

AI Order Assistant is a Smart Kitchen and higher feature. 86 Chaos can help managers build better order drafts by looking at what is already in the restaurant: current stock, par levels, pending orders, events, prep needs, Menu Intelligence links, burn/waste logs, and invoice history.

“What should I order?”

Performance Foodservice buns, chicken tenders, shredded lettuce US Foods ranch base, fryer oil, wing sauce Warnings fryer oil price up 8%, ranch usage above par

Why it matters

Ordering is where time savings and inventory control meet.

The goal is not to let AI blindly order food. The goal is to give managers a smarter draft that can be reviewed, edited, copied, saved, or applied to the normal order screen.

Stock

Smart order suggestions

Suggestions can use stock, par, pending orders, prep needs, events, Menu Intelligence links, burn/waste logs, and invoice history.

Draft

Vendor-grouped AI drafts

AI drafts group suggested items by vendor so managers can review the order the way they actually place it.

Review

Apply to the normal order screen

AI suggestions can be applied to the standard Order screen for human review before anything is finalized.

Copy

Copy the full draft

Managers can copy a full AI order draft for sharing, saving, messaging, or comparing before placing an order.

Save

Save to existing orders

AI drafts can be saved into the existing orders collection so they become part of the restaurant’s normal order workflow.

Warn

Price and par warnings

Invoice price warnings and waste/par warning cards help managers see when costs or usage patterns need attention.

Python Order Intelligence

Behind the scenes, Python Order Intelligence looks for ordering signals.

Ordering intelligence can support demand and order forecasts, confidence scores, invoice velocity, day-of-week usage signals, par recommendations, invoice price trend warnings, waste/par insights, prep forecast checks, event supply planning, and vendor draft grouping.

Safety

Drafts are not vendor orders.

AI Order Assistant can suggest, explain, copy, save, and apply drafts for review. It does not automatically send vendor orders.

Forecast

Better ordering forecasts

Python Forecast supports deeper order forecasting and reviewable ordering suggestions based on the restaurant’s real signals.

Brief

Manager attention

Ordering risks, price warnings, waste/par concerns, and stock problems can surface where managers already check the day.

Review

Manager-reviewed drafts

AI-assisted ordering creates suggestions and drafts for review, not automatic vendor orders.

Planning tools

Orders should know what the restaurant is about to need.

Events, prep prediction, menu dependencies, waste, par levels, pending orders, and invoice history all tell part of the story. AI Order Assistant pulls those signals into a manager-friendly starting point.

Event supply planning: “What do we need for Saturday’s event?” Prep prediction: spot likely prep needs before the list is short. Invoice price warnings: flag unusual vendor price changes. Waste/par warning cards: show items that may be over-prepped or under-stocked. Manager Brief AI Ordering Attention: surface ordering issues where managers already look. AI Tools launcher card: open AI Order Assistant fast from the tools area.

AI ordering workflow

1Review stock, par, pending orders, events, prep, menu links, waste, and invoice history.
2Generate vendor-grouped order suggestions.
3Show price, waste, and par warnings.
4Copy the draft, save it, or apply it to the normal Order screen.
5Manager reviews before placing the order.
