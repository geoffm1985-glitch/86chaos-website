Public vs internal

Customer-facing features are separated from internal owner tools.

The website can show the restaurant value without dumping private internal controls into the sales pitch. Owner/admin controls, security, backups, audit logs, and support tools are included, while internal super-admin workspaces stay behind the curtain.
