How it works

Start with the shift. Grow into the whole operation.

86 Chaos is designed to feel useful fast. Start with prep, 86 alerts, reminders, and schedules, then layer in inventory, financials, recipes, and maintenance as the team gets comfortable.

1

Set up the workspace

Add the restaurant, team, roster roles, prep stations, recipes, vendors, and the workflows you want to use first.

2

Run today from one hub

Use prep, tasks, reminders, 86 alerts, schedules, and team updates to keep the crew pointed in the same direction.

3

Use real app screens

Managers can move between schedule, prep, recipes, inventory, invoices, financials, and maintenance without jumping apps.

4

Improve from the records

Use history from invoices, labor, daily close, prep, burn logs, and repairs to see what keeps repeating.

MoneySales, labor, and food cost signals stay visible.
