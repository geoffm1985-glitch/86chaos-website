How it works

Start with one workflow. Expand when it proves itself.

86 Chaos does not need to replace everything on day one. Start where your restaurant leaks the most time, then add modules as the crew gets comfortable.

Step 1

Map the restaurant

Add staff, roster roles, stations, vendors, recipes, schedules, and the workflows you want beta testers using first.

Step 2

Run today

Use prep, tasks, 86 alerts, reminders, schedule, and team updates to keep the day visible to the people who need it.

Step 3

Connect the back office

Invoices, inventory, labor, daily close, repairs, and reporting start building a cleaner operating record.

Step 4

Improve the repeat problems

Use the history to see what keeps breaking: over-prep, missed close steps, repairs, high labor, or repeated outages.
