Why Smart Kitchen is the sales anchor

Smart Kitchen is the plan that can protect food cost, not just organize tasks.

AI-assisted ordering, invoice intelligence, waste/par warnings, prep prediction, recipe/menu costing signals, and menu-impact alerts attack the expensive parts of restaurant chaos: over-ordering, under-ordering, surprise stockouts, hidden waste, price jumps, and manager time.

“What should I order?” becomes a reviewable vendor draft, not a guessing game. Invoice price jumps and duplicate invoice risks become easier to catch. Waste/par insights help owners spot where food is leaking out of the building. Menu Intelligence shows which missing ingredients hurt the menu most.
