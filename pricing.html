Why the intelligence layer matters

Smart Kitchen is built for the expensive parts of restaurant chaos.

Over-ordering, under-ordering, missed vendor cutoffs, hidden waste, price jumps, weak pars, and unclear menu impact all cost money. Smart Kitchen turns those signals into manager-reviewed recommendations.

AI order drafts reduce guesswork before vendor orders are placed. Invoice price warnings make cost changes easier to catch. Waste/par insights show where food may be leaking out of the operation. Menu Intelligence shows which missing ingredients hurt the menu most.
