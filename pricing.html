AI value

AI is part of the value, not a separate science project.

Voice commands save taps. Ordering intelligence protects inventory decisions. Python scans flag cost, waste, par, labor, data health, and setup issues. The result is an app that saves time and helps managers catch expensive problems sooner.

Inventory ROI

AI ordering is part of the pay-for-itself story.

Time savings help cover the subscription. Inventory and ordering discipline can create the bigger upside. AI Order Assistant helps managers spot what to order, what not to over-order, what prices changed, and what upcoming events may require.

Smart suggestions from stock, par, pending orders, events, prep, waste, and invoices. Invoice price warnings before a cost increase becomes invisible. Waste/par warning cards for over-prep or under-stock risks. Vendor-grouped drafts that can be copied, saved, or applied to orders.
