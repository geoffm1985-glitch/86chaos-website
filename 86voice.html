Safety matters

Convenience without reckless automation.

86Voice respects permissions, plan access, workspace rules, confidence levels, and confirmation rules. It can move fast for safe actions and slow down when the action could affect the restaurant.

86 alerts ask for confirmation before sending. Shared reminders require permission and teammate matching. Ambiguous matches show review choices instead of guessing. Voice actions can be logged for accountability. Undo is available only for safe rollback actions.

How a command runs

1Transcript appears so the user can check what was heard.
286Voice detects the intent: prep, task, 86 alert, reminder, search, status, maintenance, burn log, message, or undo.
3The app matches the right item, teammate, recipe, screen, or inventory record.
4Low-confidence or risky actions ask for review or confirmation.
5The app shows the result and opens the relevant screen.
