86Voice

Voice and typed commands for real kitchen work.

86Voice helps teams use 86 Chaos without stopping the shift. It can create prep, tasks, reminders, 86 alerts, events, availability records, maintenance issues, waste logs, messages, and status questions. In Smart Kitchen and higher, it also connects to AI ordering and Menu Intelligence.

AI integration

86Voice makes the AI tools easier to use.

Managers do not have to remember where every feature lives. 86Voice can route practical questions into the right workflow: ordering drafts, menu impact, prep status, event needs, waste logs, maintenance issues, and Manager Brief-style attention items.

“Build an order draft.” “What does ranch affect?” “What prep is left?” “Log waste 4 buns.” “Report the cooler is leaking.” “What needs attention?”

Guardrails

AI helps. Managers approve.

86Voice and AI can suggest, explain, draft, alert, analyze, and report. High-risk actions stay in normal review and approval workflows.
