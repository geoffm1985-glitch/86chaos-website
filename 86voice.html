86Voice turns natural voice or typed commands into the right 86 Chaos workflow, so teams can handle prep, tasks, reminders, 86 alerts, events, availability and request-off records, maintenance, waste, Message Board posts, status questions, menu impact, and AI-assisted ordering without hunting through menus.

What 86Voice can help with

Faster actions, fewer forgotten notes.

Prep and tasksAdd or update prep, add multiple items, capture quantities, units, stations, and dates, then mark matching work done.
Reminders, events, and staffing recordsCreate personal or shared reminders, add events, and record availability or request-off information where permitted.
86 alerts and menu impactSay an item is out, ask what it affects, and route the result into the correct kitchen workflow.
AI-assisted orderingAsk “What should I order?”, “Build an order draft,” “What do we need for Saturday’s event?”, or “Add buns to the order draft.”
Status questionsAsk what needs done, what is late, what is out, what is low, or what needs a manager’s attention.
Maintenance, waste, and messagesReport equipment problems, log burn/waste, and create Message Board posts when the user has permission.

AI integration

86Voice makes the AI tools easier to use.

Managers and staff do not have to remember where every feature lives. 86Voice routes practical requests into the right workflow, making the system easier to use during a shift and reducing the “my staff will never use another app” problem.

“Build an order draft.” “What does ranch affect?” “What prep is left?” “Log waste 4 buns.” “Report the cooler is leaking.” “What needs attention?”

Guardrails

AI helps. Managers approve.

86Voice and AI can suggest, explain, draft, alert, analyze, and report. High-risk actions stay in normal review and approval workflows.
