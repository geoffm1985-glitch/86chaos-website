60+ practical capabilities

Daily manager visibility

See the shift before the shift starts chasing you.

  • Manager Brief for priorities, exceptions, and follow-up
  • Kitchen Command Center for active operational issues
  • Overdue prep, tasks, reminders, and maintenance visibility
  • Active 86 alerts and affected-menu context
  • Ordering, stock, labor, and close signals in one place
  • Role-aware views so people see the work that matters to them

Prep and task execution

Keep work assigned, visible, and moving without another clipboard.

  • Daily prep lists with quantities, units, dates, and stations
  • Smart Prep Matching to catch likely duplicates
  • One-time and recurring daily, weekly, or monthly tasks
  • Due times, categories, ownership, notes, and completion tracking
  • Line checks, opening work, closing work, and manager follow-up
  • Fast add, update, search, and mark-done workflows

AI-assisted ordering

Build a stronger draft from the signals the kitchen already creates.

  • AI Order Assistant with manager-reviewed recommendations
  • Stock, par, prep, waste, invoices, events, and vendor history inputs
  • Vendor-grouped order drafts for easier review
  • Event-aware questions such as what is needed for Saturday
  • Pending-order awareness to help avoid accidental duplication
  • Managers approve every action that changes business records

Inventory, pars, and vendors

Keep the numbers useful enough to guide the next decision.

  • Inventory items, quantities, units, locations, and notes
  • Par levels and low-stock visibility
  • Vendor records, ordering context, and grouped purchasing views
  • Order-draft review and item adjustments before approval
  • Burn and waste logging tied to real kitchen items
  • Searchable history for managers reviewing what changed

Invoices and food-cost signals

Turn paperwork into useful warnings instead of another filing pile.

  • Invoice image and PDF scanning with manager review
  • Invoice price warnings and unusual-change visibility
  • Invoice history connected to vendors and inventory context
  • Waste and burn trends that can expose recurring leaks
  • Par issues and ordering signals surfaced for follow-up
  • Higher scan and intelligence limits on Owner Pro

Menu Intelligence and recipes

Know what an ingredient affects before service finds out for you.

  • Searchable recipe book and kitchen reference
  • Ingredient-to-menu-item dependency mapping
  • 86 impact showing unavailable or affected menu items
  • Menu image and PDF scan intelligence with human review
  • Ingredient questions such as “What does ranch affect?”
  • Recipe, prep, inventory, and menu signals connected together

Scheduling and availability

Build schedules around the people and roles the restaurant actually uses.

  • Schedule builder with weekly, biweekly, and monthly workflows
  • Custom roster roles used across scheduling and reporting
  • Employee availability and request-off records
  • Availability, request-off, and coverage conflict visibility
  • Schedule templates, shift editing, and publishing
  • Employee views for upcoming shifts and schedule details

Time clock, labor, and close

Give managers a clearer view of hours and daily operating numbers.

  • Employee clock-in and clock-out workflows
  • Timesheet review and punch visibility
  • Labor totals by the restaurant’s custom roster roles
  • Hours and labor exports for manager review
  • Daily ledger and close information in the Financials area
  • Permission-aware wage and financial visibility

86Voice and text commands

Make the system fast enough to use while the kitchen is moving.

  • Ask status questions such as “What needs done?”
  • Ask menu-impact and ordering questions in plain language
  • Undo safe recent actions while approvals stay in manager review

Maintenance and equipment
