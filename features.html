Features

Built around the way kitchens actually lose time.

Every module connects to a real shift problem: what needs done, what is out, who is working, what got ordered, what broke, and what the numbers are saying.

Prep

Prep & Tasks

Food prep, line checks, daily tasks, weekly tasks, and monthly tasks with quantities, stations, due dates, and completion history.

86

86 Alerts

Out-of-stock alerts can connect inventory, kitchen, managers, and message board communication so the team does not find out from an angry guest.

Inv

Inventory & Invoices

Vendor invoices, counts, order notes, burn log, par levels, and cost signals in one place instead of buried in receipts and memory.

Sched

Time Clock & Schedule

My schedule, full schedule, request-off workflows, trade board, month view, and schedule builder access for managers.

Book

Recipe Book

Recipes and specs with yields, prep time, categories, photos, and file uploads so the kitchen standard does not live in one person’s head.

$

Financial Center

Daily close, sales, labor, COGS, prime cost, cash variance, and role-based labor reporting for owner-ready snapshots.

Fix

Maintenance Center

Track repairs, high-priority equipment issues, preventative maintenance, vendor notes, and recorded costs.

Talk

Team Communication

Message board, team updates, reminders, and shared notes keep managers from repeating the same update all shift.

Mic

86Voice

Voice commands are designed for real kitchen language: add prep, complete tasks, create 86 alerts, and search recipes faster.
