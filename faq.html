FAQ

Quick answers before the fryer timer screams.

Founder Beta should feel low-pressure, useful, and clear. Here are the answers restaurants are likely to ask first.

Is Founder Beta really free?

Yes. Founder Beta is planned for 90 days free, and no credit card is required during beta.

What happens after beta?

Founder Beta restaurants receive 50% off their selected plan for the first paid year after beta.

Can restaurants pay annually?

Yes. Annual plans are available with 2 months free. Founder Beta discounts apply to the first annual payment.

How does the referral credit work?

If a restaurant refers another restaurant that becomes a paying customer, both restaurants receive a $100 account credit after the referred restaurant has completed 60 days as a paid customer.

Is 86 Chaos replacing the POS?

No. 86 Chaos is not trying to replace the POS. It is focused on operations the POS usually does not handle well: prep, 86 alerts, reminders, schedules, recipes, inventory, daily close, maintenance, and team communication.

Is this for independent restaurants?

Yes. The product is aimed at independent restaurants, bars, grills, and small kitchen teams that want practical tools without enterprise bloat.

Will early restaurants influence the app?

Yes. Founder Beta restaurants are there to help shape what gets improved, added, and simplified before the official launch.
