FAQ

Quick answers before the fryer timer screams.

Founder Beta should feel low-pressure, useful, and clear. Here are the questions restaurants are likely to ask first.

Is Founder Beta really free?

Yes. Founder Beta is planned for 90 days free, and no credit card is required during beta.

What happens after beta?

Founder Beta restaurants receive 50% off their selected plan for the first paid year after beta.

When does beta sign-up end?

Beta sign-up ends 8/31.

Can restaurants pay annually?

Yes. Annual plans are available with 2 months free. Founder Beta discounts apply to the first annual payment.

How does the referral credit work?

If a restaurant refers another restaurant that becomes a paying customer, both restaurants receive a $100 account credit after the referred restaurant has completed 60 days as a paid customer.

What can 86Voice actually do?

86Voice can add/update prep, complete prep or tasks, create recurring tasks, create 86 alerts, answer menu-impact questions, create personal/shared/recurring reminders, open screens and recipes, search Help Center, post messages, report maintenance, start burn logs, answer status questions, summarize out-of-stock items, and undo the last safe voice action.

Does the website include everything in the app?

The All Features page now maps the current 15.0.72 app coverage: Today, 86Voice, scheduling, prep/tasks, inventory, invoice scanning, recipes, Menu Intelligence, financials, maintenance, HR/training, reminders, AI Tools, settings, audit logs, and admin controls.

What AI tools does 86 Chaos have?

86 Chaos includes 86Voice, AI Order Assistant, event supply planning, prep prediction, invoice intelligence, waste/burn analysis, par recommendations, recipe/menu costing, Menu Intelligence and 86 impact, Manager Brief intelligence, Python order/ops scans, automation, recommendation approval queues, critical alerts, diagnostics, Help Center search, and bug report admin alert support.

What does AI-assisted ordering do?

AI Order Assistant can suggest order drafts using stock, par levels, pending orders, events, prep needs, Menu Intelligence links, burn/waste logs, and invoice history. Managers can copy the draft, save it, or apply suggestions to the normal Order screen for review.

How can 86 Chaos pay for itself?

If it saves just 15 minutes a day, that is 7.5 hours per month. At $24/hour, that is about $180/month in recovered management time before counting inventory, waste, stockout, or invoice visibility improvements.

Is 86 Chaos replacing the POS?

No. 86 Chaos is not trying to replace the POS. It is focused on operations the POS usually does not handle well: prep, 86 alerts, reminders, schedules, recipes, inventory, daily close, maintenance, and team communication.

Is this for independent restaurants?

Yes. The product is aimed at independent restaurants, bars, grills, and small kitchen teams that want practical tools without enterprise bloat.

What does the beta application ask for?

The form asks for name, restaurant, restaurant address, city and state, email, phone number, best time to contact, plan interest, and the biggest operational headache you want solved first.

Will early restaurants influence the app?

Yes. Founder Beta restaurants help shape what gets improved, added, and simplified before the official launch.
