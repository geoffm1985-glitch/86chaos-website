AI-assisted ordering

Build better order drafts from the information already inside the restaurant.

AI Order Assistant can use inventory, par levels, pending orders, events, prep needs, Menu Intelligence links, burn/waste logs, invoice history, and vendor info to suggest what may need ordered.

Vendor-grouped draftsDrafts are organized the way managers actually place orders.
Explain whySuggestions can show the reason: below par, event risk, prep pressure, price change, or usage pattern.
Copy, save, or applyManagers can copy the draft, save it, or apply it to the normal Order screen.
Manager reviewAI does not automatically send vendor orders or spend money.
