AI-assisted ordering

Build better order drafts from the information already inside the restaurant.

AI Order Assistant can use current stock, par levels, pending orders, upcoming events, prep needs, Menu Intelligence links, burn/waste logs, invoice history, and vendor history to suggest what may need ordered and explain why.

Vendor-grouped draftsDrafts are organized the way managers actually place orders.
Explain whySuggestions can show the reason: below par, event risk, prep pressure, price change, or usage pattern.
Copy, save, or applyManagers can copy the draft, save it, or apply it to the normal Order screen.
Manager reviewAI does not automatically send vendor orders or spend money.

Example command

“What should I order?”

86 Chaos checks the signals, creates a draft, flags risks, and leaves the final decision to the manager.

Human-approved AI

The app can do a lot of the thinking work, but not the final approval.

86 Chaos can suggest, explain, draft, alert, analyze, scan, forecast, summarize, and report. Managers approve actions that change business records, send orders, spend money, publish schedules, alter payroll-related data, change permissions, or affect billing.
