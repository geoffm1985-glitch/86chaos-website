Main AI capabilities

The AI layer is bigger than one chatbot.

86 Chaos uses several practical AI tools, each aimed at a real restaurant problem: speed, ordering, waste, cost control, menu impact, manager attention, diagnostics, and safer follow-through.

Voice

86Voice

Spoken or typed kitchen commands for navigation, reminders, prep, tasks, 86 alerts, menu-impact questions, status summaries, events, request off, availability, maintenance, burn/waste logs, Message Board posts, and safe undo.

See 86Voice
Order

AI Order Assistant

Smart order suggestions and vendor-grouped drafts using inventory, par levels, pending orders, events, prep needs, Menu Intelligence links, burn/waste logs, invoice history, and vendor info.

See AI Ordering
Plan

Event supply + prep prediction

AI can look at upcoming events, prep patterns, menu impact, inventory risk, ordering needs, and current prep state to flag what may be needed before the rush.

Invoice

Invoice intelligence

AI/Python can help detect price jumps, duplicate invoices, pack-size changes, unusual order quantities, vendor item changes, invoice velocity, and cost trend problems.

Waste

Waste, burn, and par analysis

Spot repeated waste items, bad waste days, over-prep patterns, par changes to review, burn trends, and items that may need lower prep or lower par.

Cost

Recipe and menu costing

Python Ops Intelligence can estimate recipe/menu costs, watch food-cost warnings, and flag menu items affected by invoice-driven ingredient cost changes.

Menu

Menu Intelligence and 86 impact

Connect inventory items to menu items so managers can ask what an ingredient affects, which 86 alerts matter most, and what cannot be made when something runs out.

Brief

Manager Brief intelligence

Surface ordering attention, low stock, event order risks, waste/par warnings, invoice price warnings, items affecting many menu items, and Python scan findings.

Python

Python intelligence and automation

Scheduled scans can handle Nightly Ops Scan, Morning Manager Brief, Backup Integrity Check, Invoice Price Watcher, Event Supply Risk Check, Waste Pattern Scanner, Par Recommendations, Recipe/Menu Costing Refresh, and more.

Review

Recommendation approval queue

AI can create reviewable suggestions: par changes, duplicate invoice review, broken inventory units, event order plans, high-waste items, broken menu links, and invoice price jumps.

Alert

Critical AI/Python alerts

Critical alerts can flag backup failures, huge price jumps, duplicate invoice risk, missing event order plans, data health warnings, Python scan failures, and push system issues.

Admin

Diagnostics and Help Center support

AI diagnostics can check route readiness and environment problems, while 86Voice and AI search can help staff find Help Center answers and training guidance faster.

Built-in guardrails

AI helps. Managers approve.

The AI layer is designed to be useful without taking over business decisions. It can create reports, drafts, findings, and recommendations, but sensitive actions stay review-based.

AI can suggest par, prep, ordering, event supply, recipe/menu costing, and data cleanup changes. Humans approve before real business records are changed. AI does not send vendor orders automatically or spend money. AI does not approve payroll, publish schedules, change wages, change permissions, or silently change inventory counts.

Simple rule

AI can suggest, explain, draft, alert, and report.

Humans approve anything that changes real business records.
